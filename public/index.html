    • About Weatherford Animal Sanctuary

    Weatherford Animal Sanctuary is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating abused, neglected, abandoned, or unwanted animals. We provide food, shelter, care, and handling to all of our animals to assist in finding them compatible, loving, and permanent adoptive homes.
